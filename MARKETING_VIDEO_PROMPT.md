# KrushFlow - 10 Second Marketing Video Prompt

**Duration:** 10 seconds  
**Style:** Modern, sleek, professional with tech vibes  
**Target Audience:** Restaurant owners, inventory managers, hospitality professionals  
**Tone:** Confident, efficient, innovative

---

## Video Prompt (for AI Video Generation)

**Scene 1 (0-3 seconds):**
> A busy restaurant kitchen in chaos - chef frantically counting inventory with pen and paper, stressed expression, stacks of invoices, clipboards everywhere. Dark, cluttered, overwhelming atmosphere.

**Transition (3-4 seconds):**
> Quick flash of orange light (brand color #F97316), screen wipes to reveal...

**Scene 2 (4-7 seconds):**
> Close-up of a smartphone with KrushFlow app interface glowing. A hand holds the phone, tapping the voice recording button. Smooth, clean UI with dark theme and orange accents. Voice waveform animates as the user speaks: "Ribeyes twelve, chicken twenty, salmon eight." The app instantly displays the inventory counts updating in real-time with smooth animations.

**Scene 3 (7-10 seconds):**
> Wide shot of the same chef, now relaxed and smiling, holding the phone. Kitchen is organized, clean lighting. Text overlay appears:

**Text Overlay:**
```
KrushFlow
Intelligent Inventory Management
Voice • Vision • Done.
```

**Logo:** Orange flame icon with "KrushFlow" wordmark fades in at bottom center.

---

## Alternative Prompt (More Technical Focus)

**Scene 1 (0-2 seconds):**
> Split screen: Left side shows traditional inventory counting (slow, manual, error-prone). Right side shows KrushFlow app interface (fast, automated, accurate).

**Scene 2 (2-6 seconds):**
> Rapid sequence of KrushFlow features:
- Hand uploading prep sheet photos → instant OCR text extraction
- Voice command: "Add ten lobster tails" → count updates immediately
- Finger tapping to correct a mismatch → alias saves automatically
- Export button → CSV downloads in one tap

**Scene 3 (6-10 seconds):**
> Restaurant manager reviewing perfect inventory report on tablet, satisfied smile. Text overlay:

**Text Overlay:**
```
KrushFlow
Stop Counting. Start Speaking.
AI-Powered Inventory for Restaurants
```

## Key Visual Elements to Include

### Brand Colors
- **Primary:** Orange (#F97316) - energy, innovation
- **Background:** Dark charcoal/black - premium, professional
- **Accents:** Zinc gray (#71717A) - modern, clean

### UI Elements to Showcase
- Voice recording button (pulsing orange circle)
- Real-time count updates (smooth number animations)
- Clickable correction tokens (orange highlights)
- Clean dark-themed interface
- Prep sheet photo upload tiles

### Emotional Journey
1. **Problem:** Frustration, chaos, wasted time
2. **Solution:** Relief, efficiency, control
3. **Result:** Confidence, success, satisfaction

## Voiceover Script Options

### Option 1 (Energetic)
> "Tired of counting inventory the old way? KrushFlow uses AI voice and vision to count for you. Speak. Snap. Done. Get KrushFlow."

### Option 2 (Professional)
> "Restaurant inventory shouldn't take hours. With KrushFlow's AI-powered voice and vision technology, you're done in minutes. Intelligent inventory management starts here."

### Option 3 (Minimal - Text Only)
> No voiceover. Just on-screen text with subtle sound effects:
- Pen scratching (old way)
- Smooth "whoosh" (transition)
- Satisfying "ding" (task complete)

## Music/Sound Design

**Background Music:**
- Modern, upbeat electronic track
- 120-130 BPM
- Minimal, not overwhelming
- Builds energy from 0-10 seconds

**Sound Effects:**
- Voice recording "beep" when mic activates
- Subtle "pop" for each count update
- Satisfying "success chime" at end

## Budget Considerations

### DIY (Low Budget)
- Use stock footage from Pexels/Unsplash
- AI video generation (Runway ML, Pika Labs)
- DIY voiceover or text-to-speech
- Free music from Epidemic Sound trial

### Professional (Medium Budget)
- Hire videographer for 1-day shoot
- Professional voiceover artist (Fiverr/Voices.com)
- Licensed music from Artlist or Musicbed
- Basic motion graphics

### Premium (High Budget)
- Full production company
- Professional actors
- Custom 3D animations
- Original music composition

- **DIY:** $0-50 (stock footage + AI tools)
